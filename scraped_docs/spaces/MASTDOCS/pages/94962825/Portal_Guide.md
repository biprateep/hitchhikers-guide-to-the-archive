The [MAST Data Discovery Portal](https://mast.stsci.edu/portal/Mashup/Clients/Mast/Portal.html) is a web application for searching, exploring, and selecting for download the data products from most MAST missions and other collections. This guide will help you explore the extensive features of the Portal.
