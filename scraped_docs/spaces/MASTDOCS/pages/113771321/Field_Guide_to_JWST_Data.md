JWST data products are more complex and elaborate compared with prior-generation mission products hosted by MAST. This is in part due to the highly multiplexed science instruments on JWST (i.e., each has many configurations and operating modes), and to the much greater degree to which contemporaneous exposures are combined to form science products.
