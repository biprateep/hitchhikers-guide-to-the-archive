Column Name | Column Label | Description | Examples/Valid Values | Data Type | Units  
_fes_data_set_name_ | Data ID | Uniquely identifies an observation.; First four characters are the proposal ID, next 2 indicate a target, next 2 indicate the "visit" | e.g.: C1010301000, B0300101000, Z9104301000; Also accepted are values like C1010301, B0300101, and Z9104301; | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fs_expcnt | No. Exps | Number of exposures that make up this observation | Range: 1 to 85 | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
_fes_archive_class_ | Archive Class | DADS archive class | FUS | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_targname | Target Name | Target name (uppercase); No spaces in names | Examples: HD115071, SK-68D135, PG0749+658, LINEAR-2000WM1-22 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_ra_targ | RA (J2000) | Right Ascension (J2000) | Range: 0 to 360 deg | [ra](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees

fes_reference | Ref | The number of known literature references associated with the listed proposal ID (HST) or data set name. | null, 1, or more | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_generation_date | Generation Date | Date data was processed (in UT) | Jan 18 2000 to present | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_obsend | End Time | Date and time of the end of the observation (in UT) | Range: Sep 23 1999 to present | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_obsstart | Start Time | Observation start time | Sep 22 1999 to present | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_obstime | Exp Time | Length of exposure after screening | Range: 58 to 156,614 seconds as of 3/20/06 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | seconds  
fes_rawtime | Raw Time | Total duration of exposure | Range: 57 to 240,144.1 seconds as of 3/20/06 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | seconds  
fes_prgrm_id | Program ID | Program ID; Initial letter indicates type of program.; A = First cycle, ; B= Second cycle ...; P = PI program; Q=French PI program; X = Early Release; I= In Orbit Checkout; M=Calibration; S=Science Verification; Z=Directors Discretionary; Z9 = all goes public | Examples A001,B022,C052 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_aperture | Aperture | Aperture, The three apertures supported are the ; LWRS (30"x30"; 100% throughput), ; MDRS (4"x20"; 98% throughput) and ; HIRS (1.25"x20"; 85% throughput) ; RFPT (reference point) Light from target in target colum is not entering the spectrograph | HIRS, LWRS, MDRS, RFPT | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_archive_date | Archive Date | Date written to archive (in UT) | Range: Jan 18 2000 to present | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_release_date | Release Date | Release Date. Nominal proprietary period of 6 months which may be shortend or lengthened. Date given in UT. | Feb 23 2000 to 6 months from present | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_targ_id | Object ID | Target ID from header | Examples 01, 45, 84 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_obs_id | OBS ID | Observation ID from proposal database | Examples 01, 11, 36, 81 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
_fes_asn_id_ | Association ID | Association ID (Same as Data ID or Observation ID) | e.g. A1062121000, S6013501000 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_aper_pa | Aper Pos Angle | Positiion Angle of y axis (deg E of N) | Range: 0 to 360 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
fes_bandwid | Bandwidth | Bandwidth | Range: 1 to 90 Angstroms | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | Angstroms  
fes_centrwv | Cent Wavelength | Central Wavelength | Range: -1.5 to 1061 Angstroms | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | Angstroms  
fes_dateobs | Observation Date | Observation Date given in UT. | Sep 22 1999 to present | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_ebv | EBV | E(B-V) color excess | Range: 0 to 1.4 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_elat | Ecliptic Latitude | Ecliptic Latitude | -90 to +90 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
fes_elong | Ecliptic Longitude | Ecliptic Longitude | 0 to 360 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
fes_glat | Galactic Latitude | Galactic Latitude | Range: -90 to +90 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
fes_glong | Galactic Longitude | Galactic Longitude | Range: 0 to 360 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
fes_high_pm | High Proper Motion? | Flags high proper motion objects | T, F | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_mov_targ | Moving Target? | Flags moving targets: F=fixed target ; M=moving target | F, M | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_obs_stat | Obs Status | Science Data Processing observation status code(?) | -999 to 2 (?) | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_pr_inv_f | PI First Name | PI first name (uppercase); Use wild card |  | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |

fes_scobs_id | SCOBS ID | Observation ID from Image Header | Range: 01 to 81 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_sp_type | Spectral Type | Spectral type | Examples: B5V, O6.5IIINF, LBV(B2.5IABE), BLEND | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_src_type | Source Type | Source type. P=Point, E=Extended, C=Continuum, E=Emission line | EC, EE, PC, PE | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
fes_targtype | Target Type | not used | not used | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |

fes_wavemax | Max Wave | Max Wavelength | Range: -1 to 1106 Angstroms | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | Angstroms
