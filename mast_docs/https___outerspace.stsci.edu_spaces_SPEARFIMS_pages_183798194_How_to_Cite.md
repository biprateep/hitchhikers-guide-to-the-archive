#  [How to Cite](https://outerspace.stsci.edu/spaces/SPEARFIMS/pages/183798194/How+to+Cite)
  * Created by  ![User icon: alucy](https://outerspace.stsci.edu/s/aneund/9204/1jtwilc/_/images/icons/profilepics/anonymous.svg) [Adrian Lucy](https://outerspace.stsci.edu/display/~alucy%0A), last updated on [Apr 13, 2023](https://outerspace.stsci.edu/pages/diffpagesbyversion.action?pageId=183798194&selectedPageVersions=1&selectedPageVersions=2 "Show changes") 1 minute read 


This article explains how to acknowledge and cite the use of FIMS-SPEAR data and documentation.
Please acknowledge and cite the use of FIMS-SPEAR data. MAST suggests the following:
  * Include the acknowledgement text provided on the [Mission Acknowledgements](https://archive.stsci.edu/publishing/mission-acknowledgements) page under "FIMS-SPEAR".
  * Cite the Digital Object Identifier (DOI) for FIMS-SPEAR at MAST, [10.17909/dsbe-kj54](http://dx.doi.org/10.17909/dsbe-kj54). Useful TeX syntax for citing DOIs can be found at [the MAST DOI guide](https://archive.stsci.edu/publishing/doi) in the FAQ.
  * Consider citing the main mission papers: 
    * [Edelstein et al. 2006](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006ApJ...644L.159E/abstract)
    * [Seon et al. 2011](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2011ApJS..196...15S/abstract)  

  * If your research is based on the SPEAR specially-reduced observations of the Vela supernova remnant, consider citing [Nishikida et al. 2006](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006ApJ...644L.171N/abstract).
  * If your research is based on the FIMS extracted stellar spectra, consider citing [Jo et al. 2016](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2016MNRAS.456..417J/abstract).
