This chapter describes your options for retrieving FIMS-SPEAR data from MAST. Currently, most products are only available by direct download, while a few are available in MAST's cross-mission search services. For information on the files and their contents, see instead [Data Products](https://outerspace.stsci.edu/spaces/SPEARFIMS/pages/183798172/Data+Products).
