#  About FUSE
The **Far Ultraviolet Spectroscopic Explorer** (_FUSE_) was a space-based telescope operated The Johns Hopkins University to detect light in the far-ultraviolet portion of the spectrum. See the pages below for a comprehensive overview of the mission, a summary of the FUSE Science Results, and links to a variety of technical documents.  [Mission Overview](https://archive.stsci.edu/fuse/overview.html) [Science Summaries](https://archive.stsci.edu/fuse/scisumm/index.html) [Technical Documents](https://archive.stsci.edu/fuse/technical.html) [FUSE Instrument Handbook](https://archive.stsci.edu/fuse/ih.html) - An in-depth discussion of the _FUSE_ Instrument.  [FUSE Data Handbook](https://archive.stsci.edu/fuse/dh.html) - A discription of the FUSE data.  [ FUSE Science Programs](https://archive.stsci.edu/fuse/sciprog/index.html) - A discussion and list of the FUSE Science Programs.
