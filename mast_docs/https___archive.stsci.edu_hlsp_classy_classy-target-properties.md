##  Column Descriptions
##### Basic galaxy properties derived for the CLASSY sample.  
See [Berg+2022](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.07357) for a full overview of these properties, along with a detailed description of computation methods and references.
_Target_ - CLASSY sample name, where objects are designated by an abbreviation of their sexigesimal coordinates as JHHMM±DDMM. *Some targets are linked to their respective optical SDSS spectra.
_Alt Name_ - Alternative names for target from literature.
_RA_ - Right ascension in sexigesimal format at the J2000 epoch.
_Dec_ - Declination in sexigesimal format at the J2000 epoch.
_z_ - Redshift from optical emission lines.
_Z_ neb - Nebular oxygen abundance derived using the Te "direct" method, relative to solar metallicity (where Z☉ is defined as 12+log(O/H) = 8.69, [Asplund+2021)](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021arXiv210501661A/abstract).
_log M_ ★ - Total galaxy stellar mass in units of M☉ derived from optical + IR SED fitting.
_log SFR_ - Total galaxy star formation rate (M☉ yr-1) derived from optical + IR SED fitting.
_log sSFR_ - Total galaxy specific star formation rate (yr-1), computed as log(SFR/M★).
_n_ e - Nebular electron density (cm-3) derived from the [S II] λ6717 / λ6731 ratio.
_O_ 32 - The [O III] λ5007 / [O II] λ3727 ratio as a proxy for ionization field strength.
_E(B-V)_ - The reddening derived using the Balmer decrement and the [Cardelli et al. (1989)](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1989ApJ...345..245C/abstract) extinction law.
