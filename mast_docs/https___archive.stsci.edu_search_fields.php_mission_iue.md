Column Name | Column Label | Description | Examples/Valid Values | Data Type | Units  
_iue_data_id_ | Data ID | Observation Identifier; Camera and Image sequence number; Always 8 characters | e.g. SWP02456, LWR00501, LWP12345 | [ustring](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
_iue_go_object_name_ | GO Object Name | Original target name assigned by Guest Observer (uppercase); not homogeneous | e.g. AR PAV, AR PAVONIS; BD+75 325, BD+75 0325 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_target_name | Target Name | Target name assigned by MAST Staff using preferred NED/SIMBAD name where possible; Mixed Case | e.g. HD 36395,NGC 3918,V* Z Cam | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
_iue_ra2000_ | RA (J2000) | Right Ascension (J2000) | range 0 to 360 deg | [ra](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
_iue_dec2000_ | Dec (J2000) | Declination (J2000) | range -90 to +90 deg | [dec](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_com_exp_time | Exp Time | Commanded exposure time | range 0 - 90000 seconds | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | seconds

iue_disp | Disp | Spectrograph dispersion (high or low) | HIGH, LOW | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_aper | Aper | Spectrograph aperture used | LARGE,SMALL | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
_iue_category_ | Category | Also called Object Class. See href="/iue/manual/newsips/node159.html#IUECLAS" IUE Object Class Translations | range 0-99 | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_ra1950 | RA (B1950) | RA (B1950) Decimal Degrees | range 0 to 360 deg | [ra](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_dec1950 | Dec (B1950) | Dec (B1950) For user specified search, must be decimal degrees | range -90 to +90 deg | [dec](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_glat | Galactic Latitude | galactic latitude | range -90 to +90 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_glong | Galactic Longitude | galactic longitude | range 0 to 360 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_elat | Ecliptic Latitude | ecliptic latitude | -90 to +90 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_elong | Ecliptic Longitude | ecliptic longitude | 0 to 360 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
_iue_obs_start_time_ | Obs Start Time | Observation start time | Apr 3, 1978 - Sep 27 1996 | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
_iue_program_id_ | Program ID | Program ID.. NASA, ESA, and SERC followed different naming conventions. | e.g. PHCAL, FE176, XGBYK | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_hlsp | High-Level Science Products | Number of High Level Science Products (HLSP) available from MAST | null, 1, or more | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_reference | Ref | The number of known literature references associated with data set name. | null, 1, or more | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_eff_exp_time | Eff. Exp. Time | Effective exposure time | range 0 to 90,000 seconds | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | seconds  
iue_obs_station | Obs Station | Observing station where image was downloaded | GSFC, VILSPA | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_thda_read | THDA at Read | head amplifier temperature when image was downloaded | range -5.0 to 99.99 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_thda_start | THDA at Start | head amplifier temperature at start of exposure | range 0.0 to 29.6 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_thda_end | THDA at End | head amplifier temperature at end of exposure | range 0.0 to 29.6 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_trail_flag | Trail Flag | Trail flag; N = Not Trailed; X = Trailed along X axis; Y = Trailed along Y axis | N,X,Y | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_multiple_flag | Multiple Flag | Flags images containing multiple observations; X = along X axis; Y = along Y axis; A = along aperture length; O = along other axis; N = not multiple | A,N,O,X,Y | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_segmented_flag | Segmented Flag | flags a segmented exposure | N,Y | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_pi_name | PI Name | Name of principle investigator; Mixed Case; Use wildcard | e.g., Konda, Wilson | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_obs_name | Observer's Name | Name of person observing; All Caps; Use wildcard |  | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_go_spectype | Spectral Type | GO assigned spectral type; GSFC Observations only | Letter and number e.g. A3, K7 | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_go_lum | Luminosity | GO supplied Luminosity class; Only available for GSFC data | I, Ia, Ib, Iab, II, III, IV, V, VI | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_go_mag | Magnitude | GO supplied visual mag. | -22 to +30 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_go_bv | BV | GO designated color index (GSFC only) | range -2.00 to 9.00 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_go_ebv | EBV | GO-designated E(B-V); GSFC observations only | range -5.5 to 9.5 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_fes_mode | FES Mode | FES Mode; S=Slow; F=FAST; U=Underlap; O=Overlap; NO=; SO=; BO=Blind Offset | S,F,U,O,NO,SO,BO,FU,FO | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_fes_counts | FES Counts | FES counts (related to visual mag) | range 0 - 28673 | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_comments | Comments | Comments on image quality by RA | GSFC format: E=nnn, C=nnn, B=nnn; VILSPA used numeric form | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_uvc_voltage | UVC Voltage | UV converter voltage | -5.0 kV; -4.5 kV (LWR only) | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | kV  
iue_pos_angle | Position Angle | position angle | Range 0.0 - 360. deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_roll_angle | Roll Angle | spacecraft roll angle | Range 0 to 360 deg | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | degrees  
iue_fa_proc_ts | FA Processing Date | processing date | range Mar 18, 1993 to Jan 7, 2000 | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_sips_proc_ts | SIPS Processing Date | date of IUESIPS processing | Apr 3 1978 - Sep 19 1997 | [datetime](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_cc_percent | CC Percentage | percent of successful cross correlations | range 0.0 to 100.0 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_cc_medn | CC Median | median cross correlation coeff. | range 0.0 - 1.0 | [float](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |

iue_miss_mf | Missing Minor Frames | number of minor frames missing during transmission | range 0 - 6151 | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_microphonics | Microphonics | microphonics flag | N,Y | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |   
iue_nonstandard | Non Standard Image | flags non-standard image | N,Y | [string](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) |

iue_heater_warmup | Heater Warmup | heater warmup (only for LWR camera after 1981) | range 1 - 15 minutes | [integer](https://archive.stsci.edu/genops.html) | minutes
